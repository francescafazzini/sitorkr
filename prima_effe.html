Prima Effe

Client Feltrinelli /
2017

Feltrinelli per la Scuola
is Gruppo Feltrinelli first project
in the educational field.


Read more
We dealt with the naming and the art direction of Prima Effe visual identity with the aim of creating a fluid and dynamic system able to express all the activities offered by the project.

In order to make the system instantly recognisable and closely related to Feltrinelli logotype, we developed Prima Effe visual identity starting from the famous symbol created by Bob Noorda.
We created a fluid and dynamic identity made by four triangular elements that are free to move inside a specific grid.
This visual asset allows to reinvent the logo in different shapes and to generate a continuously evolving system.

The primary color palette includes three main bright and vibrant colors inspired by highlighters, as a reminder to the world of school in a contemporary way.
Beside them, we also kept the institutional red of Feltrinelli identity.

Both the naming and the endorsement are in New Century Schoolbook, the institutional Feltrinelli typeface, as a connection with the other Feltrinelli projects.

Concerning the naming, Prima Effe combines a reference to the world of school to the fact that it is the first group project dedicated to the educational field coordinated by the Feltrinelli and among the first ones in Italy.
