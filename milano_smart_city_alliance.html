Milano Smart City Alliance was born as a group of companies united by the desire to set up a new urban development model based on innovation, sustainability, and collaboration.

We created an identity system that could firstly convey Alliance's founding values and at the same time combine all the partners' different voices.

We played with the tones of electric blue and yellow to design a dynamic and versatile visual identity.

In addition to the color palette usage and the photographic treatment - that refers to other projects of Assolombarda, which is Alliance's leader through Assolombarda Foundation - another fundamental element of the entire visual system is the characteristic logo's M, designed through a series of pivots and moveable connections.

The entire graphic system emphasizes the concept of connection, innovation, and sharing, which drives the project.
