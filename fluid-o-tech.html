For Fluid-o-Tech’s 70th Anniversary we took care of the redesign of the brand identity, with the goal of a more contemporary and impactful effect.

The logotype is updated with the introduction of Plain family (Optimo), more legible and clear.
The chromatic palette includes, together with the new color of the emblem, different degrees of green in order to give depth to the support to be designed.

Inspired by Fluid-o-Tech’s claim “Power the flow”, the main visual is composed by a series of particles flows, where the overlapping creates dynamic and animated visual solutions.
Flows, generated from a video animation, describe sinusoidal trajectories on different levels with different widths.
The depth effect is obtained through various blur degrees of the original flows.
