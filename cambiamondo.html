Client Siram by Veolia /

A multi touchpoint educational project dedicated to 6-10 years old kids.


Read more
The assignment from Siram by Veolia, leader in energy management solutions, was to help them conceive and produce an educational long-term project.
The main value and focus of this project? To convey the values of sustainability, energy efficiency and environmental responsibility to the youngest generations.
Starting from Siram brand positioning and values, we’ve supported them developing “Cambiamondo”: a multi touchpoint educational project dedicated to 6-10 years old kids.

“Cambiamondo” has been conceived with the ambition of educating the new generations in promoting, building and managing a more sustainable world through their everyday actions.
In January 2019 the first two steps of the project will be launched: the book and the educational workshops.

Published by Gribaudo – IF – Idee Editoriali Feltrinelli, the book “Cambiamondo” includes scientific experiments, engaging artistic exercises, stickers, games, challenges and video animations, alongside with content on sustainability, specifically addressed to the main target.
